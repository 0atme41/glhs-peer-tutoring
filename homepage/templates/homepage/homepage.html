What is CSGator?

CSGator is a website created and run by computer science students at Green Level High School. The original idea was for the site to act as a portfolio for students, hosting all the amazing projects computer science students create, but the end goal has grown to include tutorials (which are currently being developed) and a discussion board for student collaboration. Projects range from games students have created to possible solutions to global problems. Our tutorials will give students the foundations for coding in Python, Java, C++, and skills for other computer science topics like web development. Here at CSGator we want to provide an opportunity for students to develop relationships with universities and connect with industry professionals. Our students have dedicated many hours to amazing projects, and we want to provide an outlet for them to show off their work.

What can CSGator do for you?

CSGator offers many resources to help students develop their knowledge of computer science and coding. We are currently creating basic computer science tutorials for students who are interested in learning about programming, and will continue to develop tutorials on more complex topics. We also offer an online message board where students can collaborate to work through computer science problems. We at CSGator are dedicated to helping our school community learn about programming and hope to be as helpful a site as possible.
